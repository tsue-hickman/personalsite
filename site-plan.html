Purpose

My goal with this website is to display my capabilities as an up-and-coming Web Developer. A little about me...

In regard to technical expertise, my wheelhouse revolves around website syntax. HTML and CSS are my most familiar languages, and I have dabbled in JavaScript and Python as well. In no way shape or form am I a coding genius, but I am consistent. My other life accomplishments attest to that. Prior to my college experience, I decided to dedicate myself for 18 months to a volunteer position as a missionary. During this time, I invested thousands of dollars that I saved throughout my high school career into serving families and individuals in the Hispanic communities throughout Iowa and Illinois. This time taught me many skills that stretched my capacity as a human being. I understood that the deepest wishes of many individuals involved in the communities that I served desired to be understood and to make their new landing spot in America their home. Growing up in a military family, I understood how it felt to be lost and out of place, and remembered longing to have someone who could provide the help I so desperately needed. This pushed me to learn and gain fluency in the Spanish language, which allowed me to connect these individuals to the church in which I served. Through this experience, my understanding of who I am flourished. My conception of who I wanted to become unveiled itself before my eyes with 4k-type clarity. Pursuing a career in web development will allow me to create sites that can help many people. The capacity of technology reaches more people than I could ever imagine reaching as a missionary, and if I can add a little improvement to their lives through my craft, then I can sleep well every night.
